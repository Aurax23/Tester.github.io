Property Description

Hillside View Estates is a prime 75-acre residential development parcel located in the rapidly growing North Austin corridor. This strategically positioned property offers panoramic hilltop views and has been pre-approved for a luxury residential community of 200+ single-family homes. The land features gentle rolling hills, mature oak trees, and natural water features.

Development Approval Status

Property has received preliminary plat approval for 212 lots with an average lot size of 1/3 acre. All major utilities are available at the property line, and environmental studies have been completed.
