Property Description

This prime 150-acre agricultural parcel is located in the heart of California's Central Valley, known as one of the most productive agricultural regions in the world. The property features Class I & II soils with excellent drainage and fertility, making it ideal for a wide range of crops including almonds, pistachios, grapes, or field crops.

Zoning & Development Potential

Zoned A-1 (Agricultural). Property has potential for conversion to vineyard, orchard development, or continued row crop production. Preliminary discussions with county planning department indicate possibility for limited residential development with proper permits.

Key Features

  • 150 contiguous acres with rectangular configuration
  • Senior water rights with established irrigation system
  • 1.2 miles of Highway 99 frontage with excellent visibility
  • Three existing water wells (two operational, one standby)
  • Storage barn (2,400 sq.ft.) and equipment shed (1,200 sq.ft.)
  • Fully fenced perimeter with gated access points
  • Level topography with less than 2% slope
